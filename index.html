⛏ Archaeological Dig Task

You are a field archaeologist on a multi-site excavation project. Over the coming weeks, your team will work four ancient dig sites — each with its own soil conditions and excavation characteristics.

You will spend 4 days at each site, digging for buried artifacts. Use your pickaxe to excavate different areas across the site each day.

At the end of each day, you'll find out whether your digging unearthed any 🏺 artifacts. Some sites are more productive than others.

This task takes about 10-15 minutes. Please stay focused throughout.

Field Instructions

① Each day, you'll arrive at a dig site. A label in the corner shows which site and which day you're on.

② Click anywhere on the screen to excavate — you may dig as many times as you like and explore different areas of the site and surrounding ground.

③ You won't see results while you dig. Wait for the amber timer bar to run out — then you'll find out if your excavation unearthed anything that day.

④ After a short break, you'll move on to your next dig day.

Different sites yield finds more often than others.
